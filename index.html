    FAQ

    Quick answers

    Is Angel Vault affiliated with Dreams Inc.?

    No! Angel Vault is a fan made tracker built as a hobby project, and is meant to be an always free resource for the Sonny Angel community. The developer does not make money from the website.

    Do I need an account?

    No. You can use the tracker without logging in to browse or temporarily see your progress, but you need to log in to save your collection after clearing browser data, switching browsers, or to see your collection on a different device.

    What’s the difference between Collected and UFS / UFT?

    Collected is for the Sonnies you’re keeping in your personal collection. UFS / UFT is for Sonnies you’re selling or trading.

    What does ISO / DISO mean here?

    ISO means “in search of.” DISO means “desperately in search of.”

    How do I request a Sonny picture or send my own photo?

    Use the Request Sonny picture option from the tracker or collected views. If you want to help, there’s also an option to upload your own photo and give permission for it to be used on the site.

    Can I use this on mobile?

    Yes. Angel Vault is designed to work on both desktop and mobile, though some views are more detailed on larger screens.

    How do I report a bug?

    Use the bug report section at the bottom of the site. You can describe the issue, add screenshots, and send it directly from there.
