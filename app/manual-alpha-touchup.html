How To Use It

  • `Restore` paints alpha back in for clipped ears, eyes, and hat details.
  • `White Paint` restores opaque white pixels, which is handy for white hats, ears, and fur.
  • `Sample Color` paints with a picked color from the PNG. Hold `Option` on Mac or `Alt` on Windows to sample from the image without leaving the brush.
  • `Erase` removes little floating bits or fringe you do not want.
  • `Alpha Mask` lets you inspect the true cutout shape without color distraction.
  • `Download PNG` exports your cleaned result; after that, I can move it into place for you if you want.
